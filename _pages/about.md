I am a PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by [Pasquale Minervini](https://neuralnoise.com/), [Emily Allaway](https://emilyallaway.github.io/) and [Ivan Titov](https://ivan-titov.org/). I am part of the [Meaning Language and Explainability (MLE) Lab](https://emilyallaway.github.io/mlegroup.html). Broadly, my research focuses on improving the trustworthiness of LLMs in complex knowledge intensive tasks, with a particular interest in making the reasoning process explainable, grounded, and verifiable. 

Previously, I worked as an AI resident at Meta AI under the supervision of [Fabio Petroni](https://www.fabiopetroni.com/), where I developed a generative framework for entity tagging that can extract entities without mention supervision. I completed my MSc in Computer Science at ETH Zurich, advised by [Shuhang Gu](https://www.en.scse.uestc.edu.cn/info/1085/2397.htm), working on image restoration and enhancement.
